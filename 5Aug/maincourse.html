Bisi bele bath It is a delicious and healthy complete meal of rice, lentils and vegetables. Bisi bele bath is one of the popular recipes from Karnataka cuisine which you can make for dinner or lunch.
Kadhi chawal The Punjabi Kadhi is thicker and creamier than other variations that have a slight thinner consistency. The main ingredients used to make the kadhi are more or less the same. The kadhi is either served with rice or with roti or phulka and usually made for lunch or dinner.
Palak paneer This delicious creamy dish is made with fresh spinach leaves, paneer (firm cottage cheese), onions, tomatoes, herbs and spices.
Brinji Rice Dunk rice, an earthy ground green masala paste, some veggies, whole spices and coconut milk in a pot, and wait till it simmers into this beautiful filling dish.
Chana masala This chana masala is made with freshly powdered spices, onions, tomatoes, herbs making the chana taste like the ones you get on the streets of Delhi. Chana masala is relished with poori or rice or roti or paratha or Naan or Kulcha.
Matar paneer spiced and creamy curry made with peas and cottage cheese. Matar paneer is one of the popular paneer recipe made in North India for lunch or dinner.
