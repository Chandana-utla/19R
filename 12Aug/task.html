Making Tea

1.Boil water in a pot

2.Add leaves and sugar

3.Let it simmer for a few minutes

4.Strain the tea into a cup and add milk if desired

Making aloo paratha

1.knead the dough for the paratha and let it rest

2.Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee.

3.For aloo curry,saute onions,tomatoes and spices in a pan.

4.Add boiled potatoes and cook until the curry thickens.

Making biryani

1.Marinate the vegetables/meat with the spices and yogurt.

2.Cook basmati rice until its 70% done.

3.Layer the marinated mat/vegetables and rice in a pot.

4.Cook on low heat(dum) until fully cooked.
